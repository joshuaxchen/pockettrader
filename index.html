App Info

Pocket Trader helps Pokémon TCG Pocket players keep track of cards they want to trade, find other players with compatible interests, and communicate about potential trades. The app is designed as a coordination tool only; card exchanges are completed by users in Pokémon TCG Pocket.

Pocket Trader is independent and fan-made. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Nintendo, The Pokémon Company, Creatures Inc., GAME FREAK, or Pokémon TCG Pocket.

Trade organization Create a profile and organize cards you have or want to find.
Player coordination Browse listings, review trader profiles, and coordinate offers.
Communication tools Use in-app messaging and notifications to follow up on trades.
Optional extras Participate in app giveaways and optional premium features where available.

Privacy Policy

Pocket Trader ("we", "our", or "us") provides an unofficial companion app for coordinating Pokémon TCG Pocket card trades. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use and share it, how long we keep it, and the choices available to you.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive account information from that provider, such as a unique account identifier and, where provided, your email address. We also store profile information you choose to add, such as a display name, Pokémon TCG Pocket friend code, avatar, and language preference.

Content you create or submit

  • Trade listings, including cards you mark as available or wanted
  • Trade offers and trade status information
  • Messages sent to other users through the app
  • Feedback, trade reactions, and card name suggestions you submit
  • Giveaway entries and related ticket activity

Usage, purchase, and device information

  • Push notification tokens and notification preferences
  • Premium subscription status and purchase entitlement information
  • Rewarded ad activity needed to determine whether a giveaway ticket was earned
  • Basic technical information needed to operate, secure, and troubleshoot the app

We do not request precise location, contacts, photos, camera access, or microphone access for Pocket Trader's trading features.

2. How We Use Information

  • Authenticate accounts and maintain user profiles

  • Coordinate trade listings, offers, chats, and related notifications
  • Provide giveaways, ticket balances, and optional rewarded ad features
  • Process, validate, and restore premium subscription entitlements
  • Respond to support requests, feedback, abuse reports, and legal requests
  • Protect the app, prevent misuse, debug issues, and improve app reliability

We do not sell your personal information. We do not request permission to track you across other companies' apps and websites for targeted advertising.

Some information you provide is visible to other users, including your display name, avatar, trade-related profile details, public listings, and information needed to coordinate a trade. Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your Pocket Trader account and associated personal data from inside the app or from outside the app.

In-App Deletion

Open Pocket Trader, go to Menu, choose Delete Account, and confirm the deletion prompt. This begins the account deletion process for the signed-in account.

Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app, email pockettradersupport@gmail.com with the subject line "Pocket Trader Account Deletion Request". Include enough information for us to identify the account, such as the sign-in email address or display name associated with the account.

Deleting your Pocket Trader account does not automatically cancel an active Apple App Store or Google Play subscription. Subscription cancellation must be managed through the platform where the subscription was purchased.

When an account is deleted, we delete or de-identify personal account data unless retention is reasonably necessary for legal compliance, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or other legitimate operational reasons.

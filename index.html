Privacy Policy

Pocket Trader  ·  Last updated: April 14, 2026

Pocket Trader ("we", "our", or "us") is an unofficial companion app for trading Pokémon TCG Pocket cards. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using Pocket Trader you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your email address and a unique identifier from that provider. We store a public-facing username, friend code, and avatar that you set yourself.

Content you create

  • Trade listings — the cards you mark as "have" or "want"
  • Chat messages sent between trading partners
  • Star ratings you leave after completing a trade
  • Feedback text submitted through the app
  • Card name translation suggestions you submit

Usage and device data

  • Language preference
  • Expo push notification token (to deliver trade and message alerts)
  • Giveaway entries and ticket balance

We do not collect precise location, contacts, photos, or any data unrelated to the trading features above.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate you and display your public profile to other traders
  • Match trade listings and facilitate card exchanges between users
  • Deliver push notifications for trades, messages, and giveaways
  • Provide customer support and improve the app based on feedback
  • Process premium subscription purchases and restore entitlements
  • Show rewarded video ads so you can earn giveaway tickets

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

Pocket Trader uses the following third-party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy.

Supabase

Our backend database and authentication provider. All user data described above is stored on Supabase servers (US West region). supabase.com/privacy

Google Sign-In

Used for account authentication. Google may share your email and profile ID with us upon sign-in. policies.google.com/privacy

Apple Sign-In

Used for account authentication on iOS. Apple may share a private relay email and user identifier with us. apple.com/legal/privacy

RevenueCat

Manages premium subscription purchases and restores. RevenueCat receives your app user ID and purchase receipts. revenuecat.com/privacy

Google AdMob

We show rewarded video ads so you can earn giveaway tickets. We request non-personalized ads only — AdMob does not build an advertising profile from your activity in Pocket Trader and we do not request the App Tracking Transparency permission. policies.google.com/privacy

Expo (Push Notifications)

We use Expo's push notification service to deliver alerts. Your device push token is stored on our servers and passed to Expo solely to deliver notifications you have enabled. expo.dev/privacy

4. Data Retention

Chat threads and their messages are automatically deleted 7 days after a trade is closed. Trade listings remain until you delete them. Account data is retained while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

5. Children's Privacy

Pocket Trader is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time in your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, email us at joshuaxinchen@gmail.com.
